## Product Scope

The app includes a dashboard-first enterprise interface, realistic manufacturing demo data, AI investigation assistant panels, RCA toolbox concepts, CAPA management, investigation quality scoring, Lean recommendations, reports, knowledge center, and settings for future enterprise configuration.

## Future AI Integration

The UI intentionally avoids exposing chain-of-thought. AI outputs are modeled around evidence considered, findings, confidence, missing data, and recommended next steps. A future backend can connect these surfaces to OpenAI APIs, retrieval over historical investigations, report generation, voice interviews, and multi-agent investigation workflows.
